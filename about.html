Our Mission

Our mission is to connect users, fair trade goods, and human rights organizations together to end modern slavery for the 45.8 million people who experience it.

LIBERATE stands to help the cause of freeing slaves through means of donating proceeds from purchases to partnered liberation organizations. It starts with you, who understands that no human deserves to be enslaved. From the goods you buy from us, the profits are given to organizations working within governments internationally to instate better worker protections and rights. These organizations also work to free individuals under the grasp of modern day slavery.


Who We Feature

LIBERATE only sells goods from fair trade companies. Fair trade is determined by our partners who certify brands that treat workers fairly to a level which meets their requirements. For more information about fair trade standards, visit here.


Where Your Money Goes

Profits made by LIBERATE go toward organizations that actively work to institute laws that give workers better treatment and rescue those trapped in the vicious cycle of slavery in the modern world.
